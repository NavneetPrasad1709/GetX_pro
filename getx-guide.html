06

Chor-Police: Fraud Rokna 🚨

Marketplace mein log cheating karte hain. Eldorado yeh handle karta hai 👇

😈 Seller cheez hi nahi deta?
Paisa locker mein hi hai — buyer ko poora refund. Seller ka kuch nahi gaya pocket se.
😈 Seller galat cheez deta hai?
Buyer "dispute" daalta hai → Eldorado proof maangta hai → galti seller ki toh paisa wapas.
😈 Buyer jhooth bolta hai "mila hi nahi"?
Chat aur delivery proof check hota hai. Jhooth pakda gaya toh buyer ka account ban!
😈 Seller chori ka account bechta hai?
ID verify ki hui hai, isliye pakda jaata hai → permanent ban + legal action.
😈 Seller ko baar-baar complaints?
Rating gir jaati hai → niche dikhta hai → kam orders → khud hi nikal jaata hai.
🫏Donkey Version: Jaise school mein har bachche ka record hota hai — kaun shararti, kaun acha. Eldorado bhi har banda track karta hai. Cheater pakde jaate hain, ache log aage badhte hain.

09

Chupe Hue Raaz 🤫

💎 Sabse bada raaz

Asli product "cheez" nahi, BHAROSA hai. Log Eldorado ko isliye paisa dete hain kyunki woh dar nahi mehsoos karte. Jo platform sabse zyada safe feel karaye, woh jeetega.

⚠️ Sabse bada risk

Payment company ban kar de (Stripe/PayPal game accounts ko risky maante hain). Isliye crypto payment backup zaroori. Yeh tumhare GETX ke liye Day-1 se sochne wali baat hai!

💸 Sabse mehnga kaam

Customer support + disputes. Har jhagde ko suljhane mein insaan lagte hain — yeh sabse zyada paisa khaata hai.

🚀 Sabse bada mauka

Naye games jaldi add karna. Jo platform naye trending game pehle add kare, woh saare buyers-sellers utha leta hai.

MVP Plan 🎯

Eldorado se yeh seedha copy karo

  • Escrow system (paisa locker) — yahi dil hai
  • Seller ID verification — chor rokne ke liye
  • Rating + reviews — bharosa banane ke liye
  • Clean fast UI — countdown timers, "5 min delivery"
  • 3-day dispute window — buyer ko safety
  • Commission model (~10-12%)

Tum inse behtar karo

  • Crypto payment Day-1 se (Eldorado ne baad mein add kiya)
  • Mobile app jaldi — gaming audience mobile pe hai
  • Sirf 5 games pe focus — quality > quantity
  • Fast Indian payment (UPI/Razorpay) add karo
  • Better support — chat bot + fast human reply

Yeh galtiyan mat karna

  • Sirf ek payment provider pe depend (ban ho gaya toh game over)
  • 150 games ek saath — tum akele ho, sambhaal nahi paoge
  • Escrow skip karna — bharosa khatam, sab chala jayega
  • ID verify na karna — chor bhar jayenge
  • Mehnga tech (.NET/Azure) abhi — paisa barbaad

Solo developer + budget kam = sirf yeh banao:
