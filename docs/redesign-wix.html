What exactly is escrow, and is it really free?
Escrow is a safe middle. When you pay, GETX holds your money instead of handing it straight to the seller. The seller only gets paid after you confirm you received exactly what was described. It is on every order, always free — never a paid add-on.
How fast is delivery?
Many listings are marked Instant and deliver in minutes through our automated system. For others, the seller delivers directly in chat. Either way, your money stays protected in escrow until you confirm.
What if the account or item isn't as described?
Open a dispute. Our AI Dispute Judge reviews the listing, chat and evidence in minutes, with humans on the edge cases. If you were wronged, your free money-back guarantee returns your funds in full.
How do you keep scammers out?
Every seller is ID-verified before they can list. Reputation, level and full sales history are public, and our trust engine continuously scores sellers. Combined with escrow, the incentive to scam simply disappears.
Which payment methods can I use?
Pay with UPI / Cards or Crypto (USDT · BTC · ETH). All checkouts run over a secure, SSL-encrypted connection, and prices are shown in USD.
Can I sell on GETX too?
Absolutely. Verify your identity, create a listing, and you're live. You get paid the moment the buyer confirms delivery — fast, fair payouts that make sellers feel like CEOs.
