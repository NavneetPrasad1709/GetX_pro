Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

Is buying a game account on GETX actually safe?
Yes. Every order is held in escrow, so your money is never sent directly to a stranger. Funds only release once you confirm you've received what was promised — and if anything's wrong, the free money-back guarantee refunds you in full.
What does the free money-back guarantee cover?
It's included on every single order at no extra charge. If the account, item or currency isn't as described — or it's never delivered — you get every dollar back. No paid add-on, no fine print.
How are sellers verified?
Sellers must pass identity verification before they can list anything. We pair that with a reputation system and an AI trust score, so the people you trade with are real and accountable.
How do I pay?
You can pay with UPI / Cards or with Crypto (USDT · BTC · ETH). Every checkout is encrypted and routed through escrow, so your payment is protected from the first click.
What happens if there's a dispute?
Open a dispute and your funds stay frozen in escrow. Our AI Dispute Judge reviews the evidence from both sides and resolves most cases within minutes — fairly, and without you chasing support for days.
