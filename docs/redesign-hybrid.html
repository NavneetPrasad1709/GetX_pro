Frequently asked

Everything you need to feel confident before your first order.

How does escrow protect my money?
When you buy, your payment is held securely by GETX — not the seller. The seller delivers, you verify, and only then do we release the funds. If the order isn't as described, you get a full refund instead. Your money is never exposed.
Is the money-back guarantee really free?
Yes — free on every single order, with no paid add-ons. If an item is never delivered or doesn't match the listing, you're entitled to a full refund. It's built into how GETX works, not sold as an extra.
How are sellers verified?
Every seller completes identity verification before they can list anything. On top of that, each seller carries a public trust score, rating and sales history, so you can always see exactly who you're buying from.
What happens if there's a dispute?
Open a dispute from your order and our AI Dispute Judge reviews the full evidence trail — chat, delivery and listing details — to reach a fair decision fast. Funds stay frozen in escrow until it's resolved, so no one can run off with your money.
How do I pay, and how fast is delivery?
Check out with UPI / Cards or Crypto (USDT · BTC · ETH). Most listings deliver instantly the moment your payment clears escrow, so you can inspect your purchase right away.
