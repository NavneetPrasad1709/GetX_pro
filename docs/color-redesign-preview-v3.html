How does escrow protect me?
When you pay, GETX holds the money — the seller doesn't receive a cent until you confirm you got exactly what you ordered. If anything's wrong, you open a dispute and your money stays protected.
What if the account gets recovered or banned?
Open a dispute within the protection window. Our AI Dispute Judge reviews the chat and proof and issues a full refund if the item wasn't as described.

Is selling on GETX safe for sellers too?
Yes. Payouts are guaranteed once the buyer confirms, and crypto/UPI settlement means no surprise chargebacks — a real advantage over card-only platforms.
