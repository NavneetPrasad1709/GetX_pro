# GETX — Founder Strategy (the "why" behind everything)

> Source: `getx-strategy.html` + `getx-guide.html`. This is our business brain.
> Before building any feature, check it serves one of these goals.

## The big idea
Eldorado/G2G/ZeusX ki copy = haarna. We **out-build** in a niche. A new marketplace only wins
if it is **10x better at ONE thing**, not 10% better at everything. Our one thing:
**fast + AI-powered trust.**

## Why new marketplaces die (avoid these)
- Copy-paste (no reason to switch) · chicken-egg (buyers without sellers) · no trust ·
  scope too big (150 games solo) · money runs out · no network effect.

## What buyers actually buy: **TRUST**, not the item.
People pay the platform because they don't feel scammed. The safest-feeling platform wins.

## Competitor weaknesses (our opening)
Slow support · painful/slow disputes · new sellers get no visibility · sellers feel like
"listing posters", not business owners. **That gap is our opportunity.**

## The 5 opportunity gaps
1. **Instant + verified auto-delivery** (real automation, not fake "5 min")
2. **AI Dispute Judge** (minutes, not days)
3. **Live Trust Score** (real-time, updates on behaviour)
4. **Seller = CEO dashboard** (profit, trends, AI pricing, demand forecast)
5. **Community layer** (guides, leaderboards — people stay without buying)

## 5-layer trust system (hard to copy)
1. ID + face verify (Sumsub) → stop scammers at the gate
2. Live Trust Score 🔴🟢 → behaviour-based, real-time
3. AI Fraud Radar → flag weird patterns instantly
4. Smart Escrow → risk-based holds, fast release for verified sellers
5. AI Dispute Judge ⚖️ → reads chat + proof, fair verdict in minutes; humans only on hard cases

## AI moats (our unfair advantage)
HIGH impact: **AI Dispute Judge · AI Fraud Radar · AI Support 24/7** — exactly the slow,
expensive work for competitors. We make it cheap + fast with Claude.
MED impact: AI price helper · AI recommendations · AI demand forecast.

## Seller lock-in (network effect)
Onboarding in 5 min (first listing free) · business dashboard · automation tools · growth
suggestions · reputation history · loyalty points · referral · creator badges · social profile.
The more a seller builds their world here, the harder to leave — even if a competitor is cheaper.

## Copy / Improve / Remove / Invent
- **Copy (proven):** escrow · ID verification · ratings/reviews · clean fast UI + delivery timers · commission model.
- **Improve:** real auto-delivery · AI 24/7 support · AI dispute (minutes) · seller business tools · lower fees.
- **Remove/avoid:** 150 games at once (do 5) · single payment provider · cluttered UI (G2G) ·
  expensive stack (.NET/Azure) · heavy seller paperwork (ZeusX).
- **Invent (the win):** AI Dispute Judge · live Trust Score · seller CEO dashboard · community layer · AI demand forecast.

## Roadmap phases (business)
- **Phase 1 · MVP (months 1-3):** solo, ~₹2-5K/mo, goal = first 100 sales. Escrow, 5 games,
  crypto+UPI, buyer-seller chat, ID verify, basic admin. Tech: Medusa + Mercur.
- **Phase 2 · Growth (months 4-12):** 1000+ active users. AI support, live trust score,
  auto-delivery, mobile, loyalty, referral.
- **Phase 3 · Leader (year 2+):** AI Dispute Judge, AI Fraud Radar, community, creator economy, more games.

## Money model
Commission (kept LOW vs Eldorado) + premium/featured listings + crypto + (future) seller-tools subscription.

## Biggest risks → mitigation
- **Payment ban** (Stripe/PayPal dislike game accounts) → 3 gateways incl. crypto from Day 1.
- **Chicken-egg** → start with ONE game (Pokemon GO), hand-seed ~10 trusted sellers, then scale.

## First step (business)
Medusa + Mercur MVP → 1 game (Pokemon GO) → seed 10 trusted sellers → prove people buy/sell → scale.
