Frequently asked

How does escrow protect me?
When you pay, GETX holds the money — the seller gets nothing until you confirm you received exactly what you ordered. If anything's wrong, open a dispute and your money stays protected.
What if the account gets recovered or banned?
Open a dispute within the protection window. Our AI Dispute Judge reviews the proof and issues a full refund (including fees) if the item wasn't as described.
How fast is delivery?
Many listings are "Instant" and deliver within seconds. Manual listings show an estimated delivery time before you buy; sellers have a delivery deadline.
Which payment methods can I use?
Cards and UPI, plus crypto (USDT, BTC, ETH). Every checkout is encrypted and escrow-backed.
How do sellers get paid?
Once the buyer confirms (or the protection window passes), funds release to the seller's wallet — withdrawable to crypto or bank. No chargebacks.
