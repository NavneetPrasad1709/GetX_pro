## What we are NOT using (and why)
- **Stripe** — game-account sellers ko ban/hold karta hai (biggest payment risk)
- **Medusa / Mercur** — solo junior MVP ke liye bahut complex; e-commerce baggage gaming me kaam ka nahi
- **Supabase** — abhi zaroorat nahi; Neon+Prisma kaafi
- **Render** — cold starts = bad buyer experience
- **Redis (at MVP)** — Vercel Cron se kaam chal jayega; baad me agar BullMQ chahiye to add karenge
